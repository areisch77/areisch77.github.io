What is Parade of Homes

Parade of homes is something very special that Hayden Homes does once a month. Hayden finds a family in need and gives them a house. Hayden thinks that giving back to the community every chance he gets is very important. Helping a family by giving them a home for free can really get them back on their feet. Hayden wants to make the worl a better place, and help anyone any chance he gets.

How to Apply

No matter what are you are in, you can go directly to a hayden home model home that is located on every sub divison and apply in person. You can always check on our social media pages as well, there will be links and directions to the model homes if you do choose to fill the application in person
